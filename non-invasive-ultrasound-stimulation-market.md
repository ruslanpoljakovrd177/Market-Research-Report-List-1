NIS is widely used in medical fields like neurology, oncology, and musculoskeletal disorders.</p><p>The Non-Invasive Ultrasound Stimulation Market is witnessing steady growth and is projected to expand at a CAGR of 5.8% during the forecast period. Secondly, the growing adoption of ultrasound technology in oncology for tumor ablation and pain management is fueling the market growth. Thirdly, advancements in ultrasound technology, such as the development of transcranial focused ultrasound (tFUS) for brain stimulation, are expanding the application of NIS and attracting more research and development investments.</p><p>In terms of market trends, there is a rising focus on developing portable and handheld ultrasound devices for enhanced patient convenience and ease of use.
