<p><p>A Pharmacy Benefit Manager (PBM) is a third-party administrator that manages prescription drug benefits for various healthcare plans. The rising costs of prescription drugs and the increasing demand for affordable healthcare services are expected to drive the growth of the market. Overall, the Pharmacy Benefit Manager (PBM) market is poised for significant growth in the coming years.</p></p>

<p><p>A Type I pharmacy benefit manager (PBM) operates as a standalone company that provides comprehensive services such as claims processing, formulary management, and pharmacy network administration. Type II PBMs, on the other hand, are typically owned and operated by health insurance companies or integrated health systems. They focus on managing prescription drug benefits for their own members or patients. Both types of PBMs play a crucial role in controlling drug costs, optimizing medication use, and ensuring efficient delivery of pharmacy services to individuals and healthcare organizations.</p></p>
