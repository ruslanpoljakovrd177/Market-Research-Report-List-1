<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Dye medical lasers are categorized into two main types: liquid dye and solid-state dye. Liquid dye lasers use a liquid medium, typically a dye solution, to generate laser light. They are known for their tunability and wide range of wavelengths. On the other hand, solid-state dye lasers use a solid-state gain medium, such as crystals or glasses, to produce laser emission. Solid-state dye lasers offer improved beam quality, higher energy output, and longer lifetimes compared to liquid dye lasers.</p></p>
