<p><p>The Electric Vehicle (EV) Chargers and Cables Market can be classified into two main types: AC Charging and DC Charging. AC Charging refers to the use of Alternating Current, where the charger converts electrical energy from the grid to charge the vehicle's battery. DC Charging, on the other hand, stands for Direct Current Charging, where the charger bypasses the vehicle's onboard charger and directly provides DC electricity to the battery. AC Charging is commonly used for home charging and slower public charging, while DC Charging enables faster charging at dedicated stations.</p></p>
