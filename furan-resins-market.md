<p><p>The furan resins market is divided into four types: furfuryl alcohol resin, furfural resin, bran ketone resin, and branone formaldehyde resin. Furfuryl alcohol resin is a versatile resin used in applications such as foundry sand binders and corrosion-resistant coatings. Furfural resin is derived from furfural and is utilized in the manufacture of friction materials and abrasive products. Bran ketone resin is a thermosetting resin employed in the production of adhesives, laminates, and coatings. Branone formaldehyde resin is a synthetic resin with applications in adhesives, coatings, and electrical insulation.</p></p>
