<p><p>Data Leak Prevention (DLP) software is a security solution designed to identify and prevent unauthorized access, transmission, or use of sensitive data within an organization. DLP software typically includes functionalities such as data classification, content inspection, policy enforcement, and incident response.</p><p>The global DLP software market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to the increasing concern over data breaches and the need for data protection. With the rising adoption of cloud computing, mobility, and the Internet of Things (IoT), organizations face challenges in securing their data across multiple endpoints and networks. This has fueled the demand for DLP solutions to safeguard against data loss and ensure compliance with regulatory requirements.</p><p>Factors driving the growth of the DLP software market include the growing awareness of data security, increasing data breaches, and stringent regulations concerning data privacy.
