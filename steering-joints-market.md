<p><p>Steering joints are essential components used in vehicles to enable movement between the steering wheel and the wheels. The market for steering joints can be classified into two types: flexible and rigid. Flexible steering joints allow for slight misalignments and vibrations, offering smooth and comfortable steering. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences in the automotive industry, ensuring efficient and reliable steering mechanisms in vehicles.</p></p>
