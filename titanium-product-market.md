<p><p>Titanium is a lustrous transition metal with excellent strength, low density, and high corrosion resistance. The aerospace sector is the largest consumer of titanium products, as this metal is widely used in aircraft structures, engine components, and fasteners. The growing global air passenger traffic and the need for fuel-efficient aircraft are driving the demand for titanium products in this industry.</p><p>Furthermore, the automotive industry is also contributing to the growth of the titanium product market. Titanium is used in exhaust systems, suspension springs, and engine components due to its lightweight and high-temperature resistance properties. The automotive industry's focus on reducing vehicle weight to improve fuel efficiency is boosting the demand for titanium products.</p><p>In the medical industry, titanium is used in orthopedic implants, dental implants, and surgical instruments due to its biocompatibility and corrosion resistance.
