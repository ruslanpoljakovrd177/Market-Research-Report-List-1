<p><p>The Die Bonding Film market comprises two main types: Non-Conductive Type and Conductive Type. Non-conductive films are used for die bonding in electronic devices where electrical insulation is required between components. These films prevent electrical current flow and ensure proper functioning of the device. On the other hand, Conductive Type films are used when electrical conductivity is needed for effective thermal dissipation and electrical connections. Both types play crucial roles in the die bonding process, ensuring the optimal performance and reliability of electronic devices.</p></p>
