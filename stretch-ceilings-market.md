PVC ceilings are made from polyvinyl chloride, a durable and cost-effective material, commonly used in residential and commercial spaces. On the other hand, PTFE ceilings are made from polytetrafluoroethylene, a high-performance material known for its exceptional durability, resistance to chemicals, and ability to withstand extreme temperatures.
