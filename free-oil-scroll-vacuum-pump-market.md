<p><p>The Free-oil Scroll Vacuum Pump market includes two main types: Single-sided Scroll and Double-sided Scroll. Single-sided Scroll pumps consist of a single spiral-shaped scroll and a stationary scroll, creating a single pocket between them. These pumps are commonly used for low to medium vacuum applications. On the other hand, Double-sided Scroll pumps possess two spiral scrolls, both rotating against each other and compressing gas in multiple pockets. These pumps are typically utilized for high vacuum applications, owing to their increased compression efficiency and lower ultimate pressure capabilities.</p></p>
