<p><p>Thioglycolic acid, also known as mercaptoacetic acid, is a clear, colorless liquid with a strong, unpleasant odor. Thioglycolic acid finds wide applications in various industries, including personal care, pharmaceuticals, textiles, and chemicals.</p><p>In the personal care industry, thioglycolic acid is widely used in hair care products, especially in hair removal creams. It acts as a reducing agent that breaks down the protein structure in hair, allowing for easy removal. In the pharmaceutical industry, it is used in the synthesis of drugs and as a stabilizer for penicillin. In the textile industry, thioglycolic acid is used in the dyeing process to improve color fastness.</p><p>The current outlook for the thioglycolic acid market is positive.
