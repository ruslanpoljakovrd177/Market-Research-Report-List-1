<p><p>Vinyl surface coatings refer to protective coatings applied to vinyl surfaces to enhance their appearance and durability. The market for these coatings can be categorized into three types: solvent-based, waterborne (latex), and powder coatings. Solvent-based coatings are made by dissolving resins and additives in organic solvents. Waterborne coatings use water as a carrier medium and are more environmentally friendly. Powder coatings are applied as dry powder and then melted to form a continuous film. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
