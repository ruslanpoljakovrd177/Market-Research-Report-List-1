These products have a longer shelf life and are convenient for transportation and storage.</p><p>Condensed Dairy Product Market: This market includes dairy products that have been reduced in volume by removing water, but still have a high concentration of sugar. Condensed dairy products are often used as ingredients in baking and cooking due to their thick and sweet nature.</p><p>Evaporated Dairy Product Market: This market consists of dairy products that have been partially dehydrated to remove water content, but retain a creamy consistency. Evaporated dairy products are often used in recipes and beverages as a substitute for fresh milk, as they have a longer shelf life and are easy to reconstitute.</p></p>
