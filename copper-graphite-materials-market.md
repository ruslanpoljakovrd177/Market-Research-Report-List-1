<p><strong>The Copper Graphite Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Blocky</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Copper graphite materials are available in two main types: powder and blocky. Copper graphite powder is finely divided and consists of copper particles coated with graphite, which provides lubrication and thermal conductivity. This type is preferred for applications such as metal processing, electrical contacts, and electrical discharge machining. Blocky copper graphite, on the other hand, is formed into solid blocks or bars and offers high mechanical strength and electrical conductivity. It is commonly used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and electronics. Both types play crucial roles in various manufacturing processes and equipment.</p></p>
