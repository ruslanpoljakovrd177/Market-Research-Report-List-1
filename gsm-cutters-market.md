<p><p>GSM cutters refer to devices that are designed to cut off GSM (Global System for Mobile Communications) signals in a particular area. These devices are available in two main market types: portable and desktop. Portable GSM cutters are smaller in size and can be easily carried around, offering convenience and flexibility. On the other hand, desktop GSM cutters are larger and mainly used in fixed locations. Both types serve the purpose of disabling GSM signals, preventing communication or unauthorized usage of mobile devices in a specific area.</p></p>
