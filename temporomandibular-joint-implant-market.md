<p><p>The Temporomandibular Joint (TMJ) Implant Market consists of two main types of components: Mandibular and Fossa components. The Mandibular component refers to the part of the implant that is placed in the lower jawbone (mandible), allowing for movement of the jaw. On the other hand, the Fossa component is inserted into the temporal bone (fossa) of the skull, providing stability and support for the joint. Together, these components enable the TMJ implant to function effectively and restore mobility to individuals with TMJ disorders.</p></p>
