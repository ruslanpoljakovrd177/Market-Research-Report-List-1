<p><p>High titanium slag is a valuable byproduct of the titanium dioxide production process. It encompasses two types: acid soluble slag and chlorination slag. Acid soluble slag is produced through the sulfuric acid method, which involves dissolving titanium-rich ores in sulfuric acid. Chlorination slag, on the other hand, is obtained from the chloride process, where titanium ore is chlorinated. Both types of high titanium slag are utilized in diverse industries such as aerospace, electronics, and pigment manufacturing due to their high titanium content, which contributes to their excellent thermal and electrical properties.</p></p>
