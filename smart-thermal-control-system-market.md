<p><p>Smart Thermal Control System is a technology that regulates and maintains thermal conditions in a controlled environment. The market includes two main types: Passive and Active Smart Thermal Control Systems. Passive systems are self-regulating and require no external power source. They rely on natural phenomena like conduction and convection to manage temperature. Active systems, on the other hand, require external power and utilize sensors and actuators to actively control temperature. These systems can quickly respond to changing conditions and efficiently manage thermal control.</p></p>
