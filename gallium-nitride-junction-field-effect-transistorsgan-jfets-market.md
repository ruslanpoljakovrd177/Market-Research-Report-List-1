The market for GaN JFETs consists of two main types: P-channel Type Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor (MOS) and N-channel Type MOS. </p><p>P-channel Type MOS refers to transistors in which the charge carriers are positively charged holes. These transistors are commonly used in applications where high voltage operation is required, such as power converters and inverters.</p><p>On the other hand, N-channel Type MOS transistors use negatively charged electrons as charge carriers. They are widely used in low-power applications like integrated circuits and mobile devices due to their superior performance characteristics and smaller size.</p><p>Both types of MOS transistors play a crucial role in the GaN JFETs market, catering to different applications and requirements.</p></p>
