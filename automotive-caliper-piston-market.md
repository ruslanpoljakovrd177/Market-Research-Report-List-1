Hydraulic disc caliper pistons are commonly utilized in modern vehicles as they provide efficient braking performance. Hydraulic drum caliper pistons are found in older vehicles and function through a mechanical linkage between the brake pedal and drum brakes. Each of these market segments offers different benefits and capabilities to meet the diverse braking needs of various types of vehicles.</p></p>
