<p><p>Corrosion protection mastics serve as coatings to prevent the degradation of metal surfaces caused by corrosion. Rubberized bitumen-based mastics are formulated with a combination of rubber polymers and bitumen, providing excellent resistance to water, UV rays, and chemicals. These mastics are commonly used for applications where flexibility is essential. On the other hand, petrolatum-based mastics are composed of petroleum jelly and solid fillers, offering superior resistance to moisture and corrosion. These mastics are typically used for underground pipelines and steel structures exposed to extreme conditions.</p></p>
