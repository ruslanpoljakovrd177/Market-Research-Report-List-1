<p><p>Coloured scanning electron microscopes (CSEMs) can be classified into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I CSEMs are designed for basic research purposes and are commonly used in academic institutions and research laboratories. These microscopes provide high-resolution imaging and are suitable for studying the surface structure of various samples. On the other hand, Type II CSEMs are more advanced and intended for industrial applications. They offer additional functionalities such as elemental analysis, chemical mapping, and quantitative analysis. Type II CSEMs are frequently utilized in sectors like materials science, nanotechnology, and quality control.</p></p>
