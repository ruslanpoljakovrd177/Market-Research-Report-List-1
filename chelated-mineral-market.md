<p><p>Chelated minerals are essential nutrients that are bonded to organic compounds like proteins, amino acids, or other organic molecules, which enhances their bioavailability and makes them easier to absorb by the body. As chelated minerals have higher absorption rates and increased stability, they are preferred over inorganic minerals in livestock and poultry feed. Moreover, growing health consciousness among consumers and the increasing prevalence of mineral deficiencies have bolstered the demand for chelated mineral-based dietary supplements.</p><p>In addition, the emerging trend of organic farming practices and the increasing adoption of precision agriculture techniques have created substantial opportunities for chelated mineral products in the agricultural sector. Chelated minerals aid in improving soil fertility and crop yield, making them highly sought after by farmers and agronomists.</p><p>The future outlook of the chelated mineral market appears promising, with a projected CAGR of 6.8% during the forecasted period.
