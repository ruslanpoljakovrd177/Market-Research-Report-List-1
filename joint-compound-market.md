<p><p>Joint compound, also known as drywall mud or drywall compound, is a building material used for finishing gypsum board joints, cornerbead, and fastener heads. One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for residential and commercial construction activities worldwide. Manufacturers are continuously focusing on developing new and improved joint compounds that offer better adhesion, faster drying times, and reduced shrinkage.
