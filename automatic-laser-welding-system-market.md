<p><p>The automatic laser welding system market can be divided into two types: semi-automatic and full-automatic. Semi-automatic systems require some manual intervention, such as loading and unloading the materials, while the welding process is automated. Full-automatic systems, on the other hand, require minimal to no manual intervention as the entire welding process is automated, including material handling. Both types of systems offer increased efficiency, accuracy, and speed compared to traditional welding methods, but full-automatic systems provide a higher level of automation and require less human involvement.</p></p>
