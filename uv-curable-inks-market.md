<p><p>The UV curable inks market is segmented into various types based on the printing methods they are used in. Offset printing UV curable inks are applied to a rubber blanket before being transferred onto the printing surface. Silkscreen printing UV curable inks are pushed through a mesh screen onto the substrate. Flexo printing UV curable inks are transferred through flexible plates and have wide applications in industries like packaging. Gravure UV curable inks are engraved onto a cylinder and then transferred to the substrate. Lastly, digital printing UV curable inks are used in digital printing machines for high-resolution printing.</p></p>
