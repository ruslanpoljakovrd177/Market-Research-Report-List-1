<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The synthetic polypropylene market is composed of various types, including isotactic polypropylene, random polypropylene, and metatactic polypropylene. Isotactic polypropylene refers to a crystalline form with highly ordered polymer chains, offering high stiffness and tensile strength. Random polypropylene consists of a combination of crystalline and amorphous regions, providing a balance between stiffness and flexibility. Metatactic polypropylene, on the other hand, has irregularly ordered polymer chains, resulting in low crystallinity and improved optical properties. Each type serves different purposes and is utilized in various industries such as packaging, automotive, and textiles.</p></p>
