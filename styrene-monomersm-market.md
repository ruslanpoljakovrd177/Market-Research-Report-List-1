<p><p>Styrene Monomer (SM) is produced through two main methods - Ethylbenzene Dehydrogenation and Ethylbenzene Oxidation. In the Ethylbenzene Dehydrogenation method, ethylbenzene is converted into styrene monomer through a chemical reaction, while in the Ethylbenzene Oxidation method, ethylbenzene is oxidized to produce styrene. Both methods have their own characteristics and advantages, and are used in different regions depending on factors such as availability of raw materials and production costs.
