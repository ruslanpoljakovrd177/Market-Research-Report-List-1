<p><p>A Business Rule Management System (BRMS) is a software tool that enables companies to define, manage, and execute business rules. The BRMS market can be divided into two types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises BRMS requires installation and maintenance within a company's own infrastructure, providing more control but requiring higher upfront costs. Cloud-based BRMS, on the other hand, operates on a remote server and can be accessed through the internet, offering scalability, flexibility, and lower initial investment. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements of businesses in managing their rules effectively.</p></p>
