<p><p>The water disinfection systems market consists of two main types: chemical disinfection and physical disinfection. Chemical disinfection involves the use of chemicals, such as chlorine, ozone, or ultraviolet light, to kill or neutralize harmful microorganisms in water. These chemicals are added to the water supply in specific doses to ensure safe drinking water. Physical disinfection methods, on the other hand, use physical processes like filtration or ultraviolet light to remove or inactivate pathogens present in the water. Both methods play a crucial role in making water safe for consumption.</p></p>
