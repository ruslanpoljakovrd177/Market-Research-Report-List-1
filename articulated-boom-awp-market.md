Construction projects and the need for maintenance and repairs in existing infrastructure create a significant market opportunity. Moreover, strict safety regulations and the emphasis on worker safety are increasing the adoption of AWPs, as they provide a safer alternative to traditional ladders and scaffolding.</p><p>Additionally, the increasing focus on renewable energy sources such as wind and solar power plants is expected to fuel the demand for AWPs.
