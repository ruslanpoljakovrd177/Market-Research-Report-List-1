<p><p>The log homes market can be categorized into two main types: hand-crafted log homes and manufactured or milled log homes. Hand-crafted log homes are built using traditional methods, where the logs are individually shaped and assembled by skilled craftsmen on site. On the other hand, manufactured or milled log homes involve the use of machinery to produce uniform logs that are then assembled to construct the home. Both types offer different advantages, with hand-crafted log homes often regarded as more personalized and unique, while manufactured log homes are typically more cost-effective and faster to build.</p></p>
