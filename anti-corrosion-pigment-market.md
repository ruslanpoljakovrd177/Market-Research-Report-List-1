<p><ul><li>Phosphates Based</li><li>Silica Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-corrosion pigment market includes two main types: phosphates based and silica based. Phosphates based pigments are composed of metal phosphates that provide excellent corrosion resistance by forming a protective barrier on the metal surface. Silica based pigments, on the other hand, contain amorphous silica particles that create a barrier against moisture and oxygen, preventing corrosion. Both types play a crucial role in protecting various metal surfaces from corrosion, extending their lifespan, and maintaining their integrity.</p></p>
