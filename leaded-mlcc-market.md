<p><p>The leaded MLCC (Multi-Layer Ceramic Capacitors) market can be categorized into different types. This type is commonly used in various applications due to its versatility and ease of soldering. The second type is the axial lead type, where the leads are attached to both ends of the capacitor and extend parallel to its body.
