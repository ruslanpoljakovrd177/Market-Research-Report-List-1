<p><p>Forestry mulchers, also known as forestry mowers or brush cutters, are heavy-duty machines used in the forestry industry to clear forests, efficiently removing trees and vegetation. <p><p>Forestry mulchers are classified into different types based on their mounting style and design. Mounted mulchers are directly attached to the front of the host vehicle, such as a tractor, and are widely used for heavy-duty land clearing. Offset mulchers, on the other hand, are mounted to the side of the vehicle, allowing for easy access to narrow areas, like roadsides or fence lines. These different market types cater to specific needs and provide effective solutions for various forestry applications.</p></p>
