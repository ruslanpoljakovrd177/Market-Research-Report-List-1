<p><ul><li>Acidic Chemical Scavenger</li><li>Alkaline Chemical Scavenger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal scavenger market is divided into two main types: acidic chemical scavengers and alkaline chemical scavengers. Acidic chemical scavengers are chemicals that are used to remove metal ions from a solution by reacting with them and forming stable complexes. They are commonly used in industries such as wastewater treatment, oil refining, and metal cleaning. On the other hand, alkaline chemical scavengers work by neutralizing acid impurities and preventing corrosion. They are commonly used in industries such as power plants and boiler systems to protect metal equipment from damage.</p></p>
