Zinc EDTA is commonly used to fortify products such as cereals, dairy products, and beverages, as it aids in preventing zinc deficiency and related health issues. Additionally, the rising awareness about water treatment and the need for wastewater management are further boosting the demand for Zinc EDTA in the water treatment industry. Zinc EDTA effectively sequesters heavy metals, helping to remove pollutants from water sources.</p><p>Furthermore, the agricultural sector is a significant consumer of Zinc EDTA. It is utilized as a micronutrient fertilizer, promoting healthy plant growth and enhancing crop yields. The expanding agricultural industry, along with the focus on sustainable farming practices, is expected to drive the demand for Zinc EDTA in the coming years.</p><p>Overall, the future of the Zinc EDTA market appears promising, primarily driven by the increasing demand for fortified foods and beverages, water treatment applications, and agriculture.
