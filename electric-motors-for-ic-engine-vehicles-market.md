AC motors, or alternating current motors, use a rotating magnetic field to convert electrical energy into mechanical energy. They are commonly used in electric vehicles due to their high efficiency and ability to operate at various speeds. DC motors, or direct current motors, convert electrical energy into mechanical energy using a fixed magnetic field. They are typically used in hybrid vehicles and provide good torque at low speeds. Both AC and DC motors play essential roles in powering IC engine vehicles.</p></p>
