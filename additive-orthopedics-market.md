<p><p>Additive Orthopedics refers to the use of additive manufacturing, also known as 3D printing, in the field of orthopedics. The increasing prevalence of orthopedic disorders and injuries, coupled with the rising geriatric population, is driving the demand for effective and personalized orthopedic solutions.
