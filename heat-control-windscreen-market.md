<p><p>The Heat Control Windscreen market consists of two types: Heated Wire Windshield and Heated Coated Windshield. The Heated Wire Windshield utilizes a network of heated wires embedded in the glass to generate heat and prevent fogging or icing. It provides efficient heat distribution and is suitable for extreme weather conditions. On the other hand, the Heated Coated Windshield incorporates a thin layer of electrically conductive film on the glass surface, which heats up to ensure a clear view. It offers a cost-effective solution with easy installation and maintenance. Both market types enhance driving safety by improving visibility during adverse weather conditions.</p></p>
