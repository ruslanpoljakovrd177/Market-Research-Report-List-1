<p><p>The Lead Acid Battery market includes various types such as VRLA (Valve-regulated Lead Acid) Battery, Flooded Battery, and Others. VRLA batteries are maintenance-free, sealed batteries commonly used in applications requiring high reliability and convenience, such as uninterruptible power systems. Flooded batteries are traditional, vented batteries that require regular maintenance due to the need for water addition. They are commonly used in industrial applications and have lower upfront costs compared to VRLA batteries. The "Others" category includes niche types like gel batteries, hybrid batteries, and lithium-ion batteries, each with their own unique advantages and applications.</p></p>
