<p><p>The overhead power transmission lines market is categorized into three types based on voltage levels. Medium voltage lines range from 1 kilovolt (KV) to 100 kilovolts (KV) and are commonly employed for regional distribution. High voltage lines, on the other hand, are above 100 KV and are predominantly employed for long-distance power transmission across countries or regions. These voltage categories help define the scope and scale of power transmission infrastructure requirements.</p></p>
