<p><p>Embryo biopsy medium is a type of culture medium that is used during an embryo biopsy procedure. During this procedure, a small sample of cells is removed from the embryo for genetic testing. The market is expected to grow at a significant rate during the forecasted period, with a projected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 6.7%. The ability to screen embryos for genetic abnormalities prior to implantation can improve the success rates of IVF procedures and reduce the risk of passing on genetic disorders to offspring.
