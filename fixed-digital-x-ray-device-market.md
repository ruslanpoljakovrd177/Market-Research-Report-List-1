<p><p>Retrofit X-ray systems refer to the process of upgrading existing analog X-ray machines with digital imaging technology. This allows healthcare facilities to transition from traditional film-based X-rays to digital images. On the other hand, new digital X-ray systems refer to fully integrated digital imaging devices that are purchased and installed separately. These systems are designed to provide high-quality images digitally without the need for film or chemical development. Both retrofit and new digital X-ray systems cater to the healthcare industry's need for efficient, accurate, and digitally stored X-ray images.</p></p>
