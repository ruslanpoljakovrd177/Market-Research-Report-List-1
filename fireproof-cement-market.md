<p><p>Fireproof cement is a type of cement that possesses high resistant properties against fire, making it suitable for use in fire-affected areas such as furnaces, chimneys, and fireplaces. The market for fireproof cement can be categorized into several types. Aluminate fireproof cement is composed predominantly of calcium aluminate and offers excellent resistance to heat and abrasion. Low Calcium Aluminate fireproof cement contains reduced levels of calcium and exhibits improved strength and durability. Calcium Magnesium Aluminate fireproof cement combines calcium and magnesium aluminate for enhanced fire resistance. The "Others" category includes various fireproof cement types that do not fall into the above categories.</p></p>
