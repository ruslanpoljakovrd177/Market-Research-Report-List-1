<p><p>The rubber diaphragm market consists of various types of diaphragms that serve different purposes. Flat diaphragms are the most common type, providing simple and effective pressure regulation. Rolling diaphragms are flexible and capable of handling extreme pressure differentials. Dish-shaped diaphragms are curved and suitable for applications requiring high deflection. Covonluted diaphragms have a convoluted pattern for increased flexibility and pressure tolerance. Lastly, there are other types that cater to specific needs and requirements. These different diaphragm types offer diverse solutions for a wide range of industries and applications.</p></p>
