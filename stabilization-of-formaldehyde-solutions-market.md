<p><p>Stabilization of formaldehyde solutions refers to the process of preventing the degradation and polymerization of formaldehyde in various industrial applications. Formaldehyde is an important chemical compound used in the production of resins, plastics, and textiles, among other products. However, it is highly reactive and prone to degradation, which can affect its stability and usefulness.</p><p>To overcome this challenge, stabilizers are added to formaldehyde solutions to prevent its degradation and extend its shelf life. These stabilizers can be either inorganic or organic in nature and work by inhibiting the polymerization reaction of formaldehyde. Stabilized formaldehyde solutions are essential in industries where long-term storage and controlled release of formaldehyde are required.</p><p>The global stabilization of formaldehyde solutions market is projected to grow at a significant rate in the coming years.
