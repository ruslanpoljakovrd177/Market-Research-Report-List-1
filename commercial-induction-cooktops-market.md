<p><p>Commercial induction cooktops are available in two types: multifunction and single function. </p><p>Multifunction cooktops offer a variety of cooking options, allowing users to perform multiple tasks simultaneously. They typically have multiple cooking zones, each with different power levels, and can handle various cooking techniques, such as simmering, boiling, grilling, and frying. These cooktops are favored by professional chefs and commercial kitchens that require versatility and efficiency in their cooking operations.</p><p>On the other hand, single function cooktops are designed for specific cooking purposes. They have a single cooking zone with a fixed power level and are primarily used for specific tasks like boiling or simmering. These cooktops are popular in commercial establishments with limited cooking requirements or as additional cooktops to complement multifunction units.</p></p>
