<p><p>The prefabricated hospital buildings market consists of two types: permanent type and relocatable type. Permanent type buildings are constructed to remain in one location indefinitely. They are typically made using durable materials and are designed to withstand long-term use. On the other hand, relocatable type buildings are assembled in a factory and then transported to different locations as needed. These buildings are often designed to be easily disassembled and reassembled, providing flexibility for healthcare providers who may require temporary or mobile facilities.</p></p>
