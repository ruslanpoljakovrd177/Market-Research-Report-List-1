<p><p>A double disc pump is a type of positive displacement pump that utilizes a unique design to handle viscous and abrasive fluids. It consists of two discs, one rotating and one stationary, with a series of flexible seals that create chambers between the discs. As the rotating disc turns, these chambers expand to draw in fluid and then contract to expel it.</p><p>The double disc pump market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
