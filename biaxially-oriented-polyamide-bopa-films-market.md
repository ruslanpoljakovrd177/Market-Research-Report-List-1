<p><strong>The Biaxially Oriented Polyamide (BOPA) Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distributed Biaxial Stretching</li><li>Synchronous Bidirectional Stretching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biaxially Oriented Polyamide (BOPA) Films are available in two types: Distributed Biaxial Stretching and Synchronous Bidirectional Stretching. Distributed Biaxial Stretching involves stretching the film uniformly from two directions, resulting in enhanced mechanical properties and increased barrier properties. Synchronous Bidirectional Stretching, on the other hand, involves stretching the film simultaneously in two directions, resulting in improved optical properties and better clarity. Both types of BOPA Films find applications in packaging, especially in food and pharmaceutical industries, due to their excellent barrier properties and durability.</p></p>
