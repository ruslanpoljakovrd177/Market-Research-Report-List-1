<p><p>The 3D Dental Scanner market is divided into two main types: 3D Dental Light Scanner and 3D Dental Laser Scanner. A 3D Dental Light Scanner uses light projection and optical sensors to create a digital model of the patient's teeth and gums. It is often used for intraoral scanning, allowing dentists to capture accurate measurements and create precise dental restorations. On the other hand, a 3D Dental Laser Scanner utilizes laser technology to scan the patient's oral cavity. It is commonly used for capturing detailed images of soft tissues and can aid in treatment planning and diagnostics. Both types of scanners offer advanced capabilities for dental professionals.</p></p>
