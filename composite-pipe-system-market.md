<p><strong>The Composite Pipe System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal Composite Pipe</li><li>Non-metallic Composite Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite pipe system market is divided into two main types: metal composite pipe and non-metallic composite pipe. Metal composite pipes consist of different layers, including an inner and outer layer made of metal such as steel, aluminum, or copper, with a middle layer made of plastic or polymer material. These pipes are used in various applications, including oil and gas, chemical, and water distribution. On the other hand, non-metallic composite pipes are made entirely of non-metal materials, such as fiberglass, plastic, or carbon fiber. This type of pipe is commonly used in industries like wastewater treatment, irrigation, and plumbing. Both metal and non-metallic composite pipes offer benefits such as corrosion resistance, high strength, and durability.</p></p>
