On the other hand, Non-UV Type is a tape that does not require UV exposure for adhesion and achieves bonding through other mechanisms. Both types are used in the semiconductor industry to protect the front side of wafers during the grinding process and ensure efficient and safe backside grinding operations.</p></p>
