<p><ul><li>Vacuum Formed</li><li>Pressure Formed</li><li>Mechanical Formed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermoforming plastic market comprises three main types: vacuum formed, pressure formed, and mechanical formed. Vacuum forming involves heating a plastic sheet and then using a vacuum to suck it onto a mold to create the desired shape. Pressure forming, on the other hand, utilizes both vacuum and pressure to improve definition and precision. Lastly, mechanical forming employs mechanical force to shape the heated plastic sheet. These three methods offer different advantages and applications within the thermoforming plastic market.</p></p>
