<p><p>The automotive roof module market consists of two main types: softtop and hardtop. Softtop roofs are made of fabric material and can be folded or retracted, giving drivers the option to have an open-air experience. They are lighter and more flexible, providing a sporty and stylish look to the vehicle. On the other hand, hardtop roofs are constructed from solid materials like metal or plastic, offering better insulation and soundproofing. They provide a more secure and luxurious feel to the car but are heavier and less versatile than softtop roofs.</p></p>
