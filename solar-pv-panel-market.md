<p><p>The Solar PV Panel market comprises three major types: Monocrystalline, Polycrystalline, and Thin-Film. Monocrystalline panels are made from a single crystal structure, giving them high efficiency but making them expensive. Polycrystalline panels are made from multiple crystal structures, offering lower efficiency and cost. Thin-Film panels are made by depositing a semiconductor material on a substrate, resulting in low efficiency but low cost and flexibility. Each market type caters to different customer demands, with monocrystalline being ideal for higher efficiency requirements, polycrystalline for cost effectiveness, and thin-film for flexible installations.</p></p>
