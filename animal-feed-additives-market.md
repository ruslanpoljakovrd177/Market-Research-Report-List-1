Amino acids are the building blocks of proteins, and they play a crucial role in animal growth and development. Vitamins are organic compounds that animals require in small amounts for various bodily functions. Enzymes are proteins that help break down complex nutrients, improving digestion and nutrient absorption in animals. These additives are added to animal feed to enhance the nutritional value and overall health of the animals.</p></p>
