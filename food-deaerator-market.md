<p><p>The food deaerator market offers three main types of equipment: spray-tray type, vacuum type, and spray type. The spray-tray type deaerator involves spraying heated water in a vertical tray to remove air and other gases. The vacuum type deaerator functions by exposing the food to low pressure, causing the gases to be released. Lastly, the spray type deaerator uses high-pressure steam to inject water into a chamber, effectively removing gases. These diverse types cater to different needs and preferences within the food industry.</p></p>
