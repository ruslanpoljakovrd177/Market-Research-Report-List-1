<p><p>The specialty polymers market consists of various types of polymers used for specific applications. Engineering Thermoplastics (ET) are polymers that possess exceptional mechanical, thermal, and chemical properties, making them suitable for industries like automotive and electronics. High-Performance Thermoplastics (HPT) are advanced polymers with superior heat resistance and mechanical strength, commonly used in aerospace and medical sectors. Specialty Films (SF) are thin polymer sheets with unique properties like high clarity or resistance to moisture, employed in packaging and construction. The "Others" category includes other specialty polymers like elastomers, adhesives, and coatings used in diverse industries.</p></p>
