<p><p>Once-through boilers (OTB) are a type of boiler that operates in a single pass, where water is continuously circulated through the system. Unlike traditional boilers, OTBs do not require a separate steam drum. Instead, they use a series of smaller diameter tubes, which heat the water as it flows through them. The resulting steam can be used for various applications, such as power generation, heating, and industrial processes.</p><p>The OTB market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for energy-efficient boilers. OTBs offer higher efficiency compared to conventional boilers, as they minimize heat losses and improve overall energy utilization. This makes them highly attractive for industries looking to reduce their energy consumption and carbon footprint.</p><p>Additionally, the growing adoption of renewable energy sources is also contributing to the market growth. OTBs can be seamlessly integrated with renewable energy systems, such as solar thermal and biomass, to maximize energy efficiency. This makes them a preferred choice for applications in the green energy sector.</p><p>Furthermore, stringent environmental regulations and the need for cleaner energy sources are propelling the demand for OTBs. This makes them compliant with environmental standards and regulations, driving their adoption across various industries.</p><p>In conclusion, the OTB market is poised for significant growth, driven by factors such as increasing demand for energy-efficient boilers, the adoption of renewable energy sources, and stringent environmental regulations.
