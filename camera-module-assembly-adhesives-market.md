<p><strong>The Camera Module Assembly Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Curable Adhesives</li><li>Non-UV Curable Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camera module assembly adhesives market consists of two main types: UV curable adhesives and non-UV curable adhesives. UV curable adhesives are designed to cure quickly when exposed to ultraviolet light, making them suitable for high-speed assembly processes. They offer excellent bond strength, temperature resistance, and durability. On the other hand, non-UV curable adhesives do not require ultraviolet light for curing and offer different curing mechanisms, such as heat, moisture, or chemical reactions. These adhesives are commonly used in applications where UV light is not feasible or desired.</p></p>
