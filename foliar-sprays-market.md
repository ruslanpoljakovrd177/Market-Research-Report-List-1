<p><p>Foliar sprays refer to the application of liquid fertilizers directly onto the leaves of plants. These sprays are classified into different market types based on their nutrient composition. Nitrogenous foliar sprays contain nitrogen as the primary nutrient and help promote plant growth and development. Phosphatic foliar sprays contain phosphorus, which aids in root development and overall plant vigor. Potassic foliar sprays provide potassium, which supports various physiological processes in plants. Lastly, micronutrient foliar sprays contain trace elements essential for plant health, such as iron, manganese, zinc, and copper.</p></p>
