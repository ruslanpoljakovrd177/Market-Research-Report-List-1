<p><p>The noise control glass market offers three main types: single glass, double glass, and triple glass. Single glass consists of a single pane that provides basic noise reduction. Double glass is made up of two panes with an insulating layer in between, offering enhanced noise control. Triple glass, the most effective option, contains three panes separated by two insulating layers, providing maximum noise reduction. These options cater to different noise control needs, with triple glass being the most efficient in reducing outside noise.</p></p>
