<p><p>The corrugated box making machine market can be categorized into two types: semi-automatic and automatic machines. Semi-automatic machines, as the name suggests, require manual intervention for certain operations, such as feeding the corrugated sheets and removing the finished boxes. On the other hand, automatic machines are fully automated, performing all operations without the need for manual assistance. These machines are equipped with advanced features like motorized controls, high-speed production, and automated box stacking. The choice between semi-automatic and automatic machines depends on factors like production volume, labor costs, and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
