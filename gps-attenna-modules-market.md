<p><strong>The GPS Attenna Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active GPS Attenna Module</li><li>Passive GPS Attenna Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS antenna modules market is divided into two types: active GPS antenna modules and passive GPS antenna modules. Active GPS antenna modules consist of a built-in amplifier that enhances the signal strength received from GPS satellites, allowing for better accuracy and performance. They require power to operate. On the other hand, passive GPS antenna modules do not have an amplifier and rely on the GPS receiver's internal amplifier for signal boosting. They do not require power and are more cost-effective. Both types serve the purpose of receiving GPS signals for positioning and navigation applications.</p></p>
