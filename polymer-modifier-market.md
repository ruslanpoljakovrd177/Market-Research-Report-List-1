<p><p>The Polymer Modifier market can be divided into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I refers to modifiers that are used in the production of engineering plastics, such as nylon and ABS. These modifiers enhance the mechanical properties of the polymers, making them stronger and more durable. On the other hand, Type II modifiers are used in the production of thermoplastic elastomers, which have both rubber-like elasticity and plastic-like processability. These modifiers improve the flexibility and impact resistance of the polymers, making them suitable for applications that require both strength and flexibility.</p></p>
