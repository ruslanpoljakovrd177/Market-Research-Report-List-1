<p><ul><li>Round Air Vents</li><li>Panel Air Vents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car air vents market is categorized into two types: round air vents and panel air vents. Round air vents are circular-shaped vents installed on the dashboard or other areas of the car interior. They allow airflow to be directed in any direction and are commonly found in older car models. On the other hand, panel air vents are rectangular-shaped vents usually integrated into the dashboard panel. They provide adjustable airflow to various parts of the car's cabin and are more commonly used in modern car designs.</p></p>
