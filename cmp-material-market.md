<p><p>CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) materials play a crucial role in the semiconductor manufacturing process. There are two main types of CMP materials: CMP Pads and CMP Slurries. CMP Pads are made of a polymeric material with a specific texture to provide mechanical polishing of the wafer surface. They are classified into different market types based on their composition and performance characteristics. On the other hand, CMP Slurries consist of an abrasive material dispersed in a liquid solution that helps remove material from the wafer surface during polishing. Various market types exist for CMP slurries based on particle size, composition, and selectivity to different materials.</p></p>
