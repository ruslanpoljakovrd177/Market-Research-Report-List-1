<p><p>The antistatic filter bag market consists of three main types: blending type, line type, and matrix type. In the blending type market, the filter bags are made by blending the antistatic fibers with other materials. The line type market involves the filtration process using linearly arranged antistatic fibers. On the other hand, the matrix type market utilizes a matrix of antistatic fibers to create the filter bags. These three types cater to different filtration requirements and offer varying levels of effectiveness in preventing static electricity buildup.</p></p>
