Circular knit fabrics are produced in a continuous tube shape using a circular knitting machine. Fair Isle knit involves a traditional knitting technique where multiple colors are used to create a patterned design. Cable knit fabrics are characterized by their twisted stitch patterns, resembling the appearance of interwoven cables. Each market type offers unique qualities and styles for different applications in the textile industry.</p></p>
