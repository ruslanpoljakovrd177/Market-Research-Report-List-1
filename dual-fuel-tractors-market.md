<p><p>Dual fuel tractors are agricultural vehicles that can operate on a combination of diesel and alternative fuels such as natural gas, biofuels, or liquefied petroleum gas (LPG). Factors driving this growth include increasing environmental concerns, government regulations promoting the use of cleaner fuels, and cost savings associated with using alternative fuels. These tractors offer reduced emissions of greenhouse gases and pollutants compared to conventional diesel tractors, making them a more sustainable choice for farmers. Additionally, the use of alternative fuels can help reduce the dependency on fossil fuels, thereby enhancing energy security.</p><p>With growing awareness about the benefits of dual fuel tractors, many agricultural equipment manufacturers have started developing and launching dual fuel models in their product portfolio. These manufacturers are focusing on improving the efficiency and performance of these tractors, resulting in improved traction, lower fuel consumption, and reduced operating costs.
