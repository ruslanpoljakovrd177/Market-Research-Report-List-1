<p><p>The Mica Cable Tape market consists of two main types: Mica Glass Fiber Tape and Mica Polyester Tape. Mica Glass Fiber Tape is a type of insulation tape that combines mica and glass fiber materials, providing excellent thermal resistance and electrical insulation. It is commonly used in high-temperature environments. On the other hand, Mica Polyester Tape is made from mica and polyester film, offering good flexibility and heat resistance. It is often utilized in low-voltage applications, such as motors and transformers, to provide electrical and thermal insulation.</p></p>
