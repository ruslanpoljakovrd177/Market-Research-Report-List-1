<p><p>CO2 Laser Coding and Marking Equipment Market includes three types of marking methods: Mask Marking, Array Marking, and Scanning Marking. </p><p>Mask Marking involves the use of a stencil or mask with pre-cut openings to create specific designs or characters on the surface to be marked. </p><p>Array Marking refers to creating an array of identical marks in a specific pattern or sequence, commonly used for barcodes or serial numbers. </p><p>Scanning Marking utilizes a high-speed laser beam that moves across the surface, creating marks in a continuous and precise manner. These market types cater to various industrial coding and marking needs.</p></p>
