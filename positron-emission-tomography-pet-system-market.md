<p><ul><li>Direct Imaging</li><li>Indirect Imaging</li><li>Alternative Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Positron Emission Tomography (PET) systems are utilized in medical imaging to visualize and diagnose various diseases. The market comprises three types: Direct Imaging, Indirect Imaging, and Alternative Imaging. Direct Imaging PET systems directly detect high-energy gamma rays produced by positron-emitting radiotracers. Indirect Imaging systems employ scintillation crystals to convert gamma rays into visible light, which is then captured by photodetectors. Alternatively, Alternative Imaging includes novel technologies like time-of-flight PET, where the time taken by photons to travel is measured for more precise imaging. These varied options cater to different medical needs and enhance the accuracy of PET imaging.</p></p>
