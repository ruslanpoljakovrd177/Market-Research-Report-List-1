<p><p>Roller mill market types are categorized into two-roller mills, four-roller mills, and other markets. Two-roller mills consist of two rollers that rotate in opposite directions, crushing materials between them. They are commonly used for grinding cereals and are affordable and easily operated. Four-roller mills, on the other hand, have four rollers that grind materials more efficiently and are often utilized in the production of flour and animal feed. Lastly, the "other" market refers to roller mills used for various purposes such as grinding minerals, chemicals, or even pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
