<p><p>The general lighting downlight market can be categorized into two types: surface-mounted and recessed. Surface-mounted downlights are installed directly on the surface of the ceiling or wall, providing general illumination without the need for a hole to be cut into the surface. On the other hand, recessed downlights are installed by creating a hole in the ceiling or wall and embedding the light fixture into it, creating a flush and seamless appearance. Both types serve the purpose of general lighting but differ in terms of installation method and aesthetic outcome.</p></p>
