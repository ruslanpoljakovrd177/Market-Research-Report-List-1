<p><strong>The Radiation Detection Materials and Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-Filled Detectors</li><li>Scintillators Detectors</li><li>Solid-State Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation detection materials and equipment market can be categorized into three main types. Gas-filled detectors use a gas medium to detect radiation, and examples include ionization chambers and proportional counters. Scintillation detectors use special crystals or liquids that emit light when radiation interacts with them, such as sodium iodide detectors. Solid-state detectors use semiconductor materials to directly convert radiation into electrical signals, such as silicon detectors. These different types of detectors offer various advantages and are suitable for different applications in radiation detection.</p></p>
