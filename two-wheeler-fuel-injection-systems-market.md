<p><strong>The Two Wheeler Fuel Injection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Fuel Injection System</li><li>Port Fuel Injection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Two Wheeler Fuel Injection Systems market consists of two types: Direct Fuel Injection System and Port Fuel Injection System. The Direct Fuel Injection System involves the direct injection of fuel into the combustion chamber of the engine, resulting in improved fuel efficiency and performance. On the other hand, the Port Fuel Injection System injects fuel into the intake port, providing a balanced air-fuel mixture for combustion. Both systems aim to deliver efficient fuel utilization, enhance engine performance, and reduce harmful emissions for two-wheeler vehicles.</p></p>
