<p><p>The organic silicone masterbatch market includes four types: white masterbatch, black masterbatch, color masterbatch, and additive masterbatch. White masterbatch is used to enhance the whiteness and opacity of plastic products. Black masterbatch is used to provide deep and consistent black color to plastic products. Color masterbatch is used to add various colors to plastic products. Additive masterbatch is used to add functional properties like UV resistance, antistatic properties, or flame retardancy to plastic products. All these types of masterbatch play a crucial role in enhancing the looks and performance of plastic products.</p></p>
