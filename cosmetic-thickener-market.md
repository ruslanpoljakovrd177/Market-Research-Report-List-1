<p><p>The cosmetic thickener market comprises two main types: powder and liquid thickeners. Powder thickeners are dry substances that, when added to cosmetic formulations, increase their viscosity, giving them a thicker and more structured consistency. They are usually made from natural or synthetic polymers. On the other hand, liquid thickeners are already in a liquid state and can be directly added to cosmetic products. They effectively enhance the texture and stability of the formulation, making them more user-friendly and appealing to consumers.</p></p>
