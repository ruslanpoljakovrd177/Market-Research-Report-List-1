<p><p>The Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) Test Meter market includes three main types of meters: Surface Resistivity Meters, Static Charge Meters, and Wrist Strap Testers. Surface Resistivity Meters are used to measure the resistivity or conductivity of surfaces to assess their susceptibility to static charge. Static Charge Meters are utilized to measure the static charge accumulated on objects or materials. Wrist Strap Testers are designed to test the functionality and effectiveness of wrist straps used to dissipate static charge from individuals to prevent ESD incidents. These meters are crucial for ensuring electrostatic safety in various industries.</p></p>
