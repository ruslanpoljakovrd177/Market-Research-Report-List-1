<p><p>Graphite yarns are widely used in various industries for their excellent properties such as high heat and chemical resistance, low thermal expansion, and electrical conductivity. These yarns are made by combining graphite flakes with binders and then spinning them into continuous fibers.</p><p>The graphite yarns market is witnessing steady growth due to their increasing demand in industries such as aerospace, automotive, electronics, and energy. In the aerospace industry, graphite yarns are used in the manufacturing of aircraft components, thermal protection systems, and rocket nozzles, as they provide excellent thermal insulation. In the automotive industry, these yarns are used in gasket manufacturing, exhaust systems, and heat shields. The electronics industry utilizes graphite yarns in the production of high-temperature wires, cables, and heating elements. Moreover, the energy sector uses graphite yarns in the manufacturing of fuel cells, solar panels, and batteries.</p><p>The market analysis suggests that the demand for graphite yarns is driven by the growing applications of these yarns in various industries.
