<p><p>The Module Photonic Integrated Circuit Market is divided into two types: Passive Photonic Integrated Circuit and Active Photonic Integrated Circuit. </p><p>Passive Photonic Integrated Circuit refers to the use of optical components such as waveguides, couplers, and filters that do not require external energy sources to manipulate light signals. It is primarily used for tasks like signal routing, multiplexing, and sensing.</p><p>In contrast, Active Photonic Integrated Circuit employs external energy sources, such as electrical current, to actively control and modulate light signals. It is commonly used in applications like optical amplification, modulation, and switching.</p></p>
