<p><strong>The High Quality PET Strapping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embossed PET Strapping</li><li>Smooth PET Strapping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Quality PET strapping is available in different types, namely embossed PET strapping and smooth PET strapping. Embossed PET strapping is characterized by its textured surface, which provides enhanced grip and prevents slipping during packaging and shipping. This type of strapping is ideal for securing heavy loads. On the other hand, smooth PET strapping has a smooth surface that allows for easy feeding through packaging machinery. It is commonly used for lighter to medium-weight loads and is known for its high tensile strength and cost-effectiveness. Both types of strapping contribute to the reliable and efficient bundling of products.</p></p>
