<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) Induction Motors market consists of two types: Single-phase Asynchronous Motors and Three-phase Asynchronous Motors. Single-phase Asynchronous Motors are utilized in NEVs where the power supply is provided as a single-phase alternating current. They are commonly found in low-power NEV applications. On the other hand, Three-phase Asynchronous Motors are used when NEVs require a higher power output and a more efficient performance. These motors run on three-phase alternating current and are suitable for medium to high-power NEV applications.</p></p>
