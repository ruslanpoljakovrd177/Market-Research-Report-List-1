<p><p>Granular sulfur, also known as elemental sulfur, is a bright yellow crystalline solid that is available in a granular form. It helps in improving the strength, elasticity, and resistance to weathering of rubber products.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing focus on clean energy sources such as solar energy is driving the demand for sulfur-based chemicals used in the production of solar panels.
