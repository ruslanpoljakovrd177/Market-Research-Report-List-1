<p><strong>The Ventilated Stretch Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Perforated Manual Film</li><li>Perforated Machine Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ventilated Stretch Film market is divided into two main types - Perforated Manual Film and Perforated Machine Film. Perforated Manual Film refers to a type of stretch film that has pre-made perforations at regular intervals, allowing for easy tearing and usage by hand. On the other hand, Perforated Machine Film is designed for use with stretch wrapping machines. It also has pre-made perforations, but the film is specifically engineered to run smoothly on automated wrapping machines, reducing the need for manual intervention.</p></p>
