<p><p>In the heavy commercial car market, automotive glass plays a crucial role in ensuring safety and durability. There are primarily three types of glass used: tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass is strengthened through a heating and cooling process, making it resistant to breakage. Laminated glass consists of two or more layers with an interlayer that provides structural integrity and prevents shattering upon impact. Other types may include acoustic glass for sound insulation or heated glass for defrosting. Each type serves a specific purpose in meeting the stringent requirements of heavy commercial vehicles.</p></p>
