<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Thermal Management Systems market includes various types of components such as thermal management module, electric fan, electric water pump, radiator, and thermostat. The thermal management module helps in controlling the temperature of the commercial vehicle's engine and cabin. Electric fans are used to cool down the engine by blowing air across the radiator. Electric water pumps circulate coolant to regulate the engine's temperature. Radiators help in removing heat from the engine coolant, while thermostats control the flow of coolant to maintain optimal temperature levels.</p></p>
