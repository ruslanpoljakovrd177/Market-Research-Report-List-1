<p><p>The Polypropylene(PP) Foam Market encompasses three main types: Extrusion Molding, Injection Foaming Molding, and Compression Molding. Extrusion Molding involves melting PP granules and shaping them through a die, resulting in a continuous foam profile. Injection Foaming Molding involves injecting molten PP into a mold, creating a solid foam structure. Compression Molding applies heat and pressure to shape PP foam, suitable for large-scale production. These different methods cater to varying requirements, allowing manufacturers to produce versatile PP foam products for various industries.</p></p>
