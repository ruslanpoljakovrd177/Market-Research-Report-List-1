<p><p>Spool valves are categorized into different types based on the materials they are made of. Stainless steel spool valves are made of stainless steel, offering durability and resistance to rust and chemicals. Plastic spool valves are made of plastic, making them lightweight and cost-effective for low-pressure systems. Carbon steel spool valves have a spool made of carbon steel, providing strength and durability in high-pressure and high-temperature environments.</p></p>
