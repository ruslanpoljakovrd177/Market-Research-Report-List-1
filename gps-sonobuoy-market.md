<p><p>The GPS Sonobuoy market consists of three main types: Active Sonobuoy, Passive Sonobuoy, and Special Purpose Sonobuoy. Active Sonobuoys emit acoustic signals that bounce off submerged objects to gather information. Passive Sonobuoys listen for underwater sounds without emitting any signals. Special Purpose Sonobuoys are designed for specific applications, such as detecting underwater vehicles or recording specific frequencies. These different types cater to various requirements and serve different purposes in maritime operations, providing valuable intelligence and enhancing situational awareness for naval forces and other users.</p></p>
