<p><p>Car alarms are security systems designed to protect vehicles from theft or unauthorized access. The passive car alarm type is an automatic system that activates when the vehicle is turned off, locking the doors and arming the alarm without the need for manual activation. On the other hand, active car alarms require the driver to manually arm the system before leaving the vehicle. Both types use various sensors to detect intrusions, such as breaking glass or motion inside the vehicle, and emit loud sounds or send alerts to the owner to deter theft.</p></p>
