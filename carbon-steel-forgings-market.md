<p><p>Carbon steel forgings refer to the process of shaping carbon steel into different shapes and forms through the application of pressure using a forging press or hammer. Carbon steel forgings are known for their high strength, durability, and resistance to wear and tear, making them ideal for applications in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, construction, and oil and gas.</p><p>The carbon steel forgings market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years.
