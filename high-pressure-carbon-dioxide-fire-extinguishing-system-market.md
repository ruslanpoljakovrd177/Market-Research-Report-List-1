<p><p>The high-pressure carbon dioxide fire extinguishing system market includes two types: the unit independent fire extinguishing system and the combined distribution fire extinguishing system. The unit independent system uses individual storage cylinders to contain carbon dioxide that can be directly discharged to extinguish fires in specific areas. On the other hand, the combined distribution system employs a network of pipes to distribute carbon dioxide to multiple areas, allowing for simultaneous fire suppression in different locations. These systems are designed to provide effective fire protection in various settings and industries.</p></p>
