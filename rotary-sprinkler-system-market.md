<p><p>The Rotary Sprinkler System market includes two main types, namely the Steam Type and the Ram Type. The Steam Type works by utilizing steam pressure to drive the rotation of the sprinkler, providing water distribution. On the other hand, the Ram Type operates by employing hydraulic pressure to power the movement of the sprinkler, ensuring efficient water distribution. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining optimal irrigation systems, delivering water evenly and effectively to various agricultural or residential areas.</p></p>
