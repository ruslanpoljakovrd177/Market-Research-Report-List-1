<p><p>The 193nm photoresist market is divided into two main types, namely dry and wet photoresists. Dry 193nm photoresists are used in a dry plasma etching process, which involves removing unwanted material from the substrate surface. It offers advantages like high resolution, low line edge roughness, and high throughput. On the other hand, wet 193nm photoresists are used in wet chemical processes, where the resist is dissolved in a liquid. Wet photoresists are known for their ease of use, good adhesion, and flexibility in various applications.</p></p>
