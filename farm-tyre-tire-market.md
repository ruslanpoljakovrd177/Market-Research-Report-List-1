<p><p>Farm Tyre (Tire) market is divided into two types: Bias Tyres and Radial Tyres. Bias Tyres are constructed with layers of cord crisscrossing at different angles, providing great traction and stability at lower speeds. They are also more affordable and durable, making them suitable for heavy-duty farming operations. On the other hand, Radial Tyres have cord layers arranged at a 90-degree angle to the tire circumference, offering improved fuel efficiency and better traction in high-speed applications. They also provide a smoother ride and reduced soil compaction on the fields.</p></p>
