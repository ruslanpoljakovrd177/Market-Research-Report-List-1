<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The smart 3D cameras market is comprised of three main types: binocular stereo vision, time of flight, and structured light systems. Binocular stereo vision involves capturing images using two lenses to create a 3D effect. Time of flight cameras use the principle of measuring the time it takes for light to travel to an object and back to calculate depth. Structured light cameras project a pattern of light onto objects and analyze the deformation of the pattern to calculate depth information. These technologies enable various applications in fields like robotics, automotive, and augmented reality.</p></p>
