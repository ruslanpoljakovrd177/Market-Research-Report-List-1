<p><p>The automotive radiator fan shroud market consists of two major types: ring type and box type. A ring type fan shroud is designed as a circular structure that surrounds the radiator fan, allowing efficient airflow and cooling. On the other hand, a box type fan shroud is a rectangular or square structure that encloses the entire radiator and fan assembly, providing a more comprehensive cooling solution. Both types of fan shrouds play a crucial role in enhancing the cooling performance of automotive radiators and maintaining optimal engine temperature.</p></p>
