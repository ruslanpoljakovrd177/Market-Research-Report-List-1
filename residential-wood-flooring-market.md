<p><p>Residential wood flooring market consists of two main types: single-layer wood flooring and multi-layer wood flooring. Single-layer wood flooring is made of a solid piece of wood, typically thicker, which provides durability and can be refinished multiple times. On the other hand, multi-layer wood flooring is composed of several layers, with the top layer being made of real wood. This type offers more versatility, as it can mimic different wood species and is often easier to install. Both types cater to the residential sector's demand for high-quality and aesthetically pleasing wood flooring options.</p></p>
