Single-side tapes have adhesive only on one side, allowing them to stick to surfaces while still being easily removable. They are preferable when only one surface needs protection. On the other hand, double-sided tapes have adhesive on both sides, enabling them to join two surfaces together temporarily or permanently. These tapes are commonly used for mounting objects or when a stronger bond is required. Both types offer convenience and flexibility in different applications.</p></p>
