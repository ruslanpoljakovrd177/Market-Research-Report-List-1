<p><p>The Silane Crosslinking Agent market comprises three types: Deacidified, Dealcoholized, and Deketoxime. In the Deacidified type, the agent is treated with a deacidifying agent to remove acid impurities. The Dealcoholized type involves removing alcohols from the agent, usually by distillation or adsorption. Lastly, the Deketoxime type is prepared by reacting the agent with an alkyl ketoxime compound. These types are important as they enhance the crosslinking process, improving the properties and performance of products such as adhesives, coatings, and sealants.</p></p>
