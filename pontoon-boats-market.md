<p><p>Pontoon boats come in two main market types based on the type of motor they use: outboard motor type and inboard motor type. In the outboard motor type, the motor is located outside the boat, at the stern, and can easily be removed or replaced. This design offers versatility and ease of maintenance. In contrast, the inboard motor type has the motor located within the boat, often in the center or rear section. This design provides better weight distribution and a quieter ride, but can be more complex to maintain and repair.</p></p>
