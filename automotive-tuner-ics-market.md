<p><p>Automotive Tuner ICs are electronic components used in automotive applications to receive and process radio frequency signals. There are two types of Tuner ICs: Hybrid/Multi-chip Integrated Circuits and Monolithic Integrated Circuits. </p><p>Hybrid/Multi-chip ICs consist of different components, such as transistors, capacitors, and resistors, that are packaged together into a single module. This allows for flexibility and customization in the design, making it suitable for complex applications. </p><p>On the other hand, Monolithic ICs are integrated circuits where all the components are fabricated on a single semiconductor wafer. This results in a more compact and cost-effective solution, suitable for simpler tuner applications.</p></p>
