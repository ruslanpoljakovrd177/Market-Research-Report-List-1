<p><p>The AC Expansion Valve market includes three types: C Type Thermostatic Expansion valve (TXM Valve), G Type Thermostatic Expansion valve (TXV Valve), and V Type Thermostatic Expansion valve (TMX Valve). The C Type uses a temperature sensing element to control refrigerant flow, maintaining a constant superheat. The G Type offers a more precise control of superheat by utilizing a pressure balance. Lastly, the V Type incorporates several improvements to enhance performance and efficiency, including a combined sensing element for both temperature and pressure control.</p></p>
