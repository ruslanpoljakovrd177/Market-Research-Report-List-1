<p><p>The automotive pillar parts market can be categorized into four types: A Pillar, B Pillar, C Pillar, and D Pillar. The A Pillar refers to the vertical support structure on either side of the windshield, connecting the roof and the dashboard. The B Pillar is the vertical support between the front and rear doors. The C Pillar connects the roof and the rear panels, typically found in four-door vehicles. The D Pillar is the vertical support at the rear of the vehicle, connecting the roof and the trunk or tailgate. These pillar parts provide structural strength and support to the vehicle's body.</p></p>
