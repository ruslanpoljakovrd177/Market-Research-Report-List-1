<p><ul><li>Colorimetric Gas Detector Tubes</li><li>Dosimetric Gas Detector Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas detector tube market includes two types of tubes - colorimetric gas detector tubes and dosimetric gas detector tubes. Colorimetric gas detector tubes use a chemical reaction to detect and measure the concentration of a specific gas. They produce a color change in the presence of the target gas. On the other hand, dosimetric gas detector tubes determine the cumulative exposure of hazardous gases over a period of time. They are typically used for personal monitoring and can be worn by workers in potentially dangerous environments. These tubes provide reliable and easy-to-read results.</p></p>
