<p><p>Spiral ultrafiltration membrane is a type of filtration technology that uses a spiral wound configuration to separate particles and solutes from a liquid or gas stream. It is widely used in various industries such as food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and water and wastewater treatment.</p><p>The global spiral ultrafiltration membrane market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
