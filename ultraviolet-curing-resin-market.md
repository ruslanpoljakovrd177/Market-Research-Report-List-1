<p><p>Ultraviolet (UV)-Curing Resin Market refers to the industry that produces UV-curable resins used in various applications. Radiation Curing, a type of UV-curing resin market, involves the exposure of resin coatings to UV light to initiate the curing process rapidly. On the other hand, Cation Curing is another type where the curing process is initiated by cationic photoinitiators instead of UV light. Both types offer quick and efficient curing, making them popular in industries such as adhesives, coatings, and inks, due to their reduced curing time and improved properties.</p></p>
