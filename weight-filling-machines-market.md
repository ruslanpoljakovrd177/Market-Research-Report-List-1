<p><p>Weight filling machines are commonly used in industries to accurately fill products by weight. There are two main types of weight filling machines in the market: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic machines are fully automated and require minimal human intervention, offering high efficiency and productivity. They can handle large volumes of products and are suitable for continuous production lines. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention in the filling process. They are more flexible and suitable for smaller production volumes or for products that require frequent changes in filling parameters.</p></p>
