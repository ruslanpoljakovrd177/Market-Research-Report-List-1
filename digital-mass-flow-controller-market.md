<p><strong>The Digital Mass Flow Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Pressure Type</li><li>Differential Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital mass flow controller market includes two main types: thermal pressure type and differential pressure type. The thermal pressure type utilizes a heated element to measure and control the flow of gas or liquid based on the heat transfer principle. On the other hand, the differential pressure type uses pressure sensors to measure the pressure difference across an obstruction, which is then used to calculate and regulate the flow rate. Both types are used in various industries for accurate and precise control of fluid flow in processes.</p></p>
