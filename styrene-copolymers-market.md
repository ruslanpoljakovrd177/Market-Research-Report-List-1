<p><p>Styrene copolymers are a group of thermoplastic resins used in various industries. Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) is a popular type known for its high impact resistance and versatility. Acrylonitrile Styrene Acrylate (ASA) offers improved weatherability and UV resistance, making it suitable for outdoor applications. Styrene Acrylonitrile (SAN) copolymers provide excellent transparency and heat resistance, often used in packaging and electrical equipment. Methyl Methacrylate Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (MABS) combines the properties of ABS and SAN, offering impact resistance and excellent aesthetics. Other styrene copolymers include styrene butadiene copolymers (SBC), styrene methyl methacrylate copolymers (SMMA), and more diverse variations for specific applications.</p></p>
