<p><p>Concealed Weapon Detection System Market includes three types: Millimeter Wave Body Scanners, X-ray Body Scanners, and Terahertz Body Scanners. Millimeter Wave Body Scanners use electromagnetic waves to detect concealed weapons on a person's body. X-ray Body Scanners employ X-rays to identify hidden objects under clothing. Terahertz Body Scanners use terahertz radiation to recognize concealed weapons through clothes without emitting ionizing radiation. These advanced technologies help security personnel detect potential threats in high-security areas, such as airports, prisons, and government buildings.</p></p>
