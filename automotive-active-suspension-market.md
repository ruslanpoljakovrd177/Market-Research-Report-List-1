<p><p>The automotive active suspension market consists of various types, including air suspension, hydraulic suspension, electromagnetic suspension, and electro-hydraulic suspension. Air suspension utilizes compressed air to support the vehicle's weight, providing a smoother and more comfortable ride. Hydraulic suspension utilizes hydraulic fluid pressurized by a pump to control the vehicle's suspension movement. Electromagnetic suspension uses electromagnets to adjust the suspension stiffness, improving both comfort and vehicle stability. Lastly, electro-hydraulic suspension combines elements of both hydraulic and electromagnetic suspension systems for enhanced performance and ride quality. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of consumers.</p></p>
