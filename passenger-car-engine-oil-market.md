<p><p>Passenger car engine oil market is divided into two types, mineral oil and synthesis oil. Mineral oil is derived from naturally occurring crude oil and undergoes minimal processing. It provides basic lubrication and is more affordable. On the other hand, synthesis oil is artificially produced using chemical reactions. It offers advanced lubricating properties and superior engine performance. Synthesis oil tends to be more expensive compared to mineral oil. The choice between the two depends on factors like budget, engine requirements, and desired performance.</p></p>
