<p><h1>Heat Soaked Glass Market Size, Market Share and Global Market Analysis Report, 2023 - 2030</h1></p><p><strong>Heat Soaked Glass Market Analysis and Latest Trends</strong></p>
<p><p>Heat soaked glass is a type of tempered glass that has undergone an additional heat treatment process known as heat soaking. This process aims to reduce the risk of spontaneous glass breakage caused by nickel sulfide (NiS) inclusions present in the glass.</p><p>The heat soaking process involves placing the tempered glass panels in a heat soaking oven for a specific duration at a predetermined temperature. This causes the NiS inclusions to expand and break, minimizing the possibility of glass breakage in the future.</p><p>The heat soaked glass market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Increasing demand for tempered glass in various applications such as construction, automotive, and electronics is one of the key factors driving market growth. The growing focus on safety and security in buildings and infrastructure projects further drives the demand for heat soaked glass.</p><p>The automotive industry also contributes to market growth as heat soaked glass is widely used in side and rear windows to enhance safety and reduce the risk of injuries from shattered glass. Additionally, the growth of the electronics industry, with the increasing use of touch screen displays and glass panels in smartphones and tablets, further fuels the demand for heat soaked glass.</p><p>In terms of regional analysis, Asia Pacific is expected to hold a significant share in the heat soaked glass market.
