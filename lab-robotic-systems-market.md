<p><p>The lab robotic systems market includes two major types: robotic arms and track robots. Robotic arms are flexible and versatile machines that perform various tasks with ease, such as handling and moving lab equipment. They have multiple joints and a gripper for manipulating objects. On the other hand, track robots are wheeled systems that follow a predefined path or track for transportation purposes. These robots are commonly used in automated laboratories to transport samples and other materials efficiently. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing lab automation and improving productivity.</p></p>
