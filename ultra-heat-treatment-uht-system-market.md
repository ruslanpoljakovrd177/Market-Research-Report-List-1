<p><p>The Ultra Heat Treatment (UHT) System market consists of two main types: Direct Heating UHT System and Indirect Heating UHT System. In a Direct Heating UHT System, the product is heated directly using steam injection or other direct heat transfer methods, ensuring quick and efficient heating. On the other hand, an Indirect Heating UHT System involves heating the product indirectly through a heat exchanging process, where the product comes in contact with a heated surface. Both types are widely used in the food and beverage industry for processing and preserving various liquid products.</p></p>
