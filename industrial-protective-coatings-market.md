<p><p>The industrial protective coatings market can be categorized into three types. Firstly, water-borne coatings are environmentally friendly and have low VOC emissions, making them increasingly popular in various industrial applications. Secondly, solvent-borne coatings use organic solvents as a base, providing excellent adhesion and durability. However, they release high levels of VOCs, making them less preferable from an environmental standpoint. Lastly, other types of coatings include powder coatings, which are known for their durability and resistance to abrasion, chemicals, and corrosion.</p></p>
