<p><p>The automotive headliner market consists of two main types: polyurethane (PU) foam and fiber materials. On the other hand, fiber material headliners are constructed using non-woven or knitted fabrics. These headliners are known for their durability, abrasion resistance, and cost-effectiveness. Both types of headliners are widely used in the automotive industry to enhance the interior aesthetics and comfort of vehicles.</p></p>
