T102 represents medium basicity calcium petroleum sulphonate and is primarily utilized in various lubricant applications. On the other hand, T103 corresponds to low basicity calcium petroleum sulphonate, extensively used in metal processing and as a rust inhibitor. These market types cater to different industry needs and play crucial roles in their respective applications.</p></p>
