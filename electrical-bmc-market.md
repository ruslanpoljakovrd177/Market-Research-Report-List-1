<p><p>Electrical BMC (Bulk Molding Compound) is a material used in electrical devices and components due to its excellent insulation and mechanical properties. There are two main types of BMC in the market: Epoxy Resin BMC and Vinyl Resin BMC. Epoxy Resin BMC is known for its high chemical resistance and dimensional stability. It is commonly used in applications requiring superior electrical insulation.
