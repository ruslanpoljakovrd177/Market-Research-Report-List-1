<p><p>Dunnage paper bags are classified based on their ply count, which refers to the number of layers of paper used for construction. In the below 2 ply market, bags are made with one or two layers, suitable for relatively lighter goods. The 2-4 ply market offers slightly increased strength for moderate weight applications. For heavier items, the 5-7 ply market provides enhanced durability. Lastly, the above 7 ply market caters to extremely heavy loads, demanding the highest level of strength and protection.</p></p>
