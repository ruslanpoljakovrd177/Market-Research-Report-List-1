<p><p>YAG Transparent Ceramic is a type of ceramic material that is increasingly being used in various applications such as optics, lasers, and sensors. It can be classified into two market types: Rare Earth Doping and Undoping. </p><p>In Rare Earth Doping, the YAG ceramic is infused with rare earth elements like neodymium or erbium. This process helps enhance the material's optical properties, such as its ability to emit or amplify light, making it suitable for laser applications.</p><p>On the other hand, in Undoping, the YAG ceramic is not mixed with any rare earth elements. This type of YAG ceramic is often used as a transparent window material due to its exceptional optical clarity and high thermal conductivity. It finds applications in industries like aerospace and defense for protective armor and optical windows.</p></p>
