<p><p>The prop-shaft assembly consists of various components that help transmit torque from the engine to the wheels of a vehicle. The rear axle assembly connects the propeller shaft to the wheels, transmitting power and supporting the weight of the vehicle.</p><p>The prop-shaft assembly market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecasted period.
