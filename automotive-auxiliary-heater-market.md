<p><strong>The Automotive Auxiliary Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fuel Based Auxiliary Heaters</li><li>Electrical / PTC Auxiliary Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive auxiliary heater market can be divided into two main types: fuel-based auxiliary heaters and electrical/PTC auxiliary heaters. Fuel-based auxiliary heaters, as the name suggests, use fuel to generate heat. They are typically powered by gasoline or diesel and are commonly found in vehicles with internal combustion engines. On the other hand, electrical/PTC auxiliary heaters rely on electricity and utilize Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) technology to generate heat. These heaters are often used in electric or hybrid vehicles as they can efficiently heat the cabin without requiring the engine to run.</p></p>
