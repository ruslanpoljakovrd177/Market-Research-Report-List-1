<p><p>Metal cutting fluids are used in the manufacturing industry to improve the cutting process and extend the tool's life. Emulsion metal cutting fluids are made of oil and water, providing good cooling and lubrication. Semi-synthetic metal cutting fluids are a combination of oil and water, offering better performance than emulsion fluids. Synthetic metal cutting fluids are entirely man-made and offer high lubrication and cooling properties. Neat oil metal cutting fluids are pure oils without any water content, offering excellent lubrication but less cooling. Each type caters to different cutting needs.</p></p>
