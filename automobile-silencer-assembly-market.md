<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Automobile Silencer Assembly market consists of two main types: Absorption Silencer and Reflection Silencer. Absorption Silencer works by absorbing the sound energy produced by the automobile engine through sound-absorbing materials, reducing the overall noise level. On the other hand, Reflection Silencer reflects the sound waves back towards the source, canceling out the noise. Both types serve to reduce vehicle noise emissions, ultimately providing a quieter driving experience and contributing to environmental noise pollution mitigation.</p></p>
