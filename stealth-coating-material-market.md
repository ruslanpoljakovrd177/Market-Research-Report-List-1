<p><p>The stealth coating material market includes various types such as epoxy, polyurethane, polyimide, and others. Epoxy is a common type of stealth coating material that provides excellent adhesion, chemical resistance, and durability. Polyurethane is known for its versatility, as it can be applied to different surfaces and offers good abrasion resistance.
