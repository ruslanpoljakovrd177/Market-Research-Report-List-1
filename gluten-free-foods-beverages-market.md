<p><p>Gluten-free foods and beverages are products that are free from gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and other grains. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing prevalence of celiac disease and gluten sensitivities among the global population has created a demand for gluten-free products. Additionally, the expanding retail network, both online and offline, has made gluten-free products more accessible and available to a wider audience.</p><p>The market for gluten-free foods and beverages is witnessing several latest trends. One key trend is the rising popularity of gluten-free bakery products, such as bread, cookies, cakes, and pastries.
