<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The hot melt granule market can be categorized into two main types: EVA-base and PUR-base. EVA-base hot melt granules are made from ethylene vinyl acetate, a copolymer known for its excellent adhesion properties. It is commonly used in packaging, woodworking, and bookbinding applications. On the other hand, PUR-base hot melt granules are made from polyurethane, a versatile polymer with excellent bonding strength. PUR-base granules are widely used in automotive, construction, and textile industries. Both EVA-base and PUR-base hot melt granules offer strong adhesion and reliable performance in various applications.</p></p>
