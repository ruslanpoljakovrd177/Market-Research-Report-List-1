<p><p>The Fabry–Pérot Etalon is an optical device used to measure the wavelength of light or to create narrowband optical filters. <p><p>Fabry–Pérot Etalon is used in various applications such as telecommunications, spectroscopy, and lasers. Solid Etalon consists of two parallel mirrors with a solid material in between, providing high stability. Air Spaced Etalon has a gap between the two mirrors, allowing for adjustments and better control of the transmitted wavelength. Piezo Tunable Etalon incorporates piezoelectric elements to manipulate the mirror separation, enabling wavelength tuning. Gires–Tournois Etalon incorporates additional coatings on the mirrors to enhance reflectivity and reduce losses, resulting in a broader spectral range.</p></p>
