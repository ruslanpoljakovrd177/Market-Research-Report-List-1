<p><p>The mild steel channel market consists of two main types: U channel and C channel. U channel is shaped like a letter "U" and is commonly used in construction and engineering for structural support. It provides strength and stability to beams and columns. C channel, on the other hand, is shaped like a letter "C" and is often used for framing and support applications. Both U channel and C channel are made from mild steel, which is a low carbon steel known for its strength and versatility.</p></p>
